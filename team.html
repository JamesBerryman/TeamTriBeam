Meet the Team

Our team is based in a small shop in Newport on the Isle of Wight, we have a group of players that play at the shop on the regular basis and as such we consider all of those people members of Team Tri-Beam! However for things such as Events we have a few select members that form Team A of Team Tri-Beam. You can find out more about our team below.
